Layer 2 — Activation Pipeline (What happens to every agent output)
Risk Tiers
Low + high confidence → auto-approved & dispatched immediately
Medium (prospect-facing, SMS) → waits in approval queue for human review
High (ads, cold close, <50% confidence) → escalated, requires explicit sign-off
📄
1 — Artifact Created
Agent output is wrapped in a standard contract: type, audience, intent, confidence score, and content.
email note crm_update report ad sms
⚖️
2 — Moral Gate & Risk Check
Checks AIBOS principles. Derives risk tier from content type, audience, and confidence score.
auto-approved pending escalated
🧑‍💼
3 — Approval Queue
Pending & escalated artifacts wait here. You can approve or reject each one via the API dashboard.
1-click approve reject + reason
🚀
4 — Dispatch & Receipt
Approved artifacts are sent to the right channel. Every send produces a timestamped delivery receipt in the database.
delivered failed
↓ Dispatched to the right channel

# Post-Demo → No-Close Nurture Sequence

**Business**: The AI Phone Guy
**Trigger**: Demo attended but no sale within 48 hours
**Goal**: Recover 15%+ of stalled demos into closed deals
**Skill**: `email-sequence` + `marketing-psychology` (loss aversion + social proof)

---

## Sequence

| # | Send day | Angle | Subject |
|---|---------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Day 2 | Recap — what we covered | quick recap + the numbers we pulled |

## Rules

- If any email gets a reply → automation pauses, Tyler handles manually
- If Email 4 gets no response → move to long-term nurture (quarterly check-in)
- If Option 2 reply → add to suppression list, never contact again
- Track close-rate lift vs. control (demos with no follow-up sequence)

## Core Framework

**Every touch uses**: Trigger → Insight → Ask

- **Trigger**: A specific, verifiable thing happening in their business or industry
- **Insight**: One non-obvious thing about AI in auto retail they haven't heard
- **Ask**: Interest-based, not a calendar book

**Every subject line**: 2–4 words, lowercase, internal-looking. The inbox test is "would a GM's assistant delete this?" If yes, rewrite.

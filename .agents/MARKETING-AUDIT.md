# Marketing Audit — All Three Businesses

**Audit Date**: 2026-03-17
**Auditor**: Marketing Skills Framework (33 skills applied)
**Scope**: Zoe (AI Phone Guy), Sofia (Calling Digital), Chase (Automotive Intelligence)

---

## Executive Summary

All three marketing agents have strong personas and clear funnel ownership, but they share common gaps: **no lead magnets, no email nurture sequences, no landing page CRO, no referral programs, and no structured content repurposing system**. The daily task definitions are solid for content ideation but miss critical conversion infrastructure. Below are prioritized improvements per business with the specific marketing skills that address each gap.

---

## Business 1: The AI Phone Guy (Zoe + Tyler + Jennifer)

### What's Working
- Full-funnel awareness → consideration → conversion structure
- Hyper-local DFW targeting is a strong differentiator
- Tyler→Zoe feedback loop (field objections → content) is smart
- Jennifer's retention focus with NPS and churn KPIs

### Critical Gaps & Recommendations

#### 1. No Lead Magnets (Skill: `lead-magnets`)
**Gap**: Zoe generates content but there's no gated asset to capture emails from interested prospects before they're ready for a demo.
**Fix**: Create 2-3 lead magnets targeted at local service business owners:
- "The Missed Call Calculator" — interactive tool showing revenue lost to voicemail (`free-tool-strategy`)
- "5-Minute Phone Audit Checklist" — PDF download for business owners to self-assess
- "Case Study Pack: How 3 DFW Businesses Stopped Losing Calls" — gated PDF

#### 2. No Email Nurture Sequence (Skill: `email-sequence`)
**Gap**: Tyler sends cold SMS and books demos, but there's no automated email sequence for prospects who aren't ready yet.
**Fix**: Build three sequences:
- **Lead Magnet → Demo**: 5-email nurture after download (educate → case study → objection → offer)
- **Post-Demo No-Close**: 4-email follow-up for prospects who attended demo but didn't buy
- **New Client Onboarding**: 7-email sequence reinforcing ROI and driving to first "saved call" moment (Jennifer's domain)

#### 3. Cold SMS Needs Cold Email Best Practices (Skill: `cold-email`)
**Gap**: Tyler's SMS outreach is strong but doesn't follow proven cold email frameworks for follow-up sequences.
**Fix**: Apply the cold-email skill's follow-up cadence to Tyler's GHL sequences:
- 3-5 touch sequence with angle rotation (not "just checking in")
- Each follow-up adds new value: stat, case study, objection answer
- Interest-based CTAs ("Worth a quick look?") instead of hard meeting asks

#### 4. No Landing Page CRO (Skill: `page-cro` + `copywriting`)
**Gap**: Zoe plans content and CTAs but there's no mention of dedicated landing pages for campaigns.
**Fix**: Create conversion-optimized landing pages for:
- Google Ads campaigns (message-match to ad copy)
- Lead magnet download pages
- Demo booking page with social proof, objections FAQ, and single CTA

#### 5. No Referral Program (Skill: `referral-program`)
**Gap**: Jennifer targets "maximum referrals" but there's no structured program.
**Fix**: Launch a client referral program:
- Offer: 1 month free for each successful referral
- Simple mechanism: unique referral link in monthly ROI reports
- Jennifer triggers referral ask after first "big win" moment

#### 6. SEO/AEO Needs Structure (Skills: `seo-audit`, `ai-seo`, `schema-markup`)
**Gap**: Zoe targets SEO/AEO keywords but there's no structured audit process.
**Fix**:
- Run initial SEO audit on theaiphoneguy.com using the seo-audit framework
- Implement schema markup for local business, FAQ, and review structured data
- Optimize content for AI search (AEO/GEO) using the ai-seo skill's content patterns

#### 7. No A/B Testing Framework (Skill: `ab-test-setup`)
**Gap**: No mention of testing landing pages, email subject lines, or SMS hooks.
**Fix**: Set up systematic A/B tests for Tyler's SMS openers and Zoe's landing pages.

## Business 2: Calling Digital (Sofia + Marcus + Carlos)

### What's Working
- 50 pieces/month content target is ambitious and measurable
- Bundle strategy (digital → AI upsell) is well-defined
- Sofia↔Nova collaboration on AI education content is smart
- Carlos's upsell radar and structured reporting

### Critical Gaps & Recommendations

#### 1. No Content Repurposing System (Skill: `social-content`)
**Gap**: Sofia targets 50 pieces/month but has no structured repurposing workflow.
**Fix**: Apply the social-content skill's repurposing system:
- 1 blog post → LinkedIn carousel + Twitter thread + Instagram reel + email snippet
- Batch 2-3 hours weekly using the content calendar template
- This turns 12 blog posts/month into 48+ pieces across channels

#### 2. No Lead Magnets for Agency Prospects (Skill: `lead-magnets`)
**Gap**: Marcus does consultative selling with free website audits, but there's no scalable lead capture.
**Fix**: Create lead magnets that pre-qualify agency clients:
- "Website Grader" — automated audit tool that scores their site (`free-tool-strategy`)
- "The SMB Digital Marketing Playbook" — comprehensive guide with self-assessment
- "AI Readiness Quiz for Business Owners" — segments leads for digital vs. AI consulting

#### 3. No Email Sequences Beyond Outreach (Skill: `email-sequence`)
**Gap**: No post-audit nurture, no client onboarding emails, no AI education drip.
**Fix**: Build four sequences:
- **Post-Audit Nurture**: 6-email sequence after free website audit (findings → solutions → bundle offer)
- **New Client Onboarding**: 7-email series setting expectations and driving to first results
- **AI Education Drip**: 5-email sequence warming existing clients for Nova's consulting
- **Monthly Newsletter**: Automated digest of Sofia's best content

#### 4. Cold Outreach Needs Frameworks (Skill: `cold-email`)
**Gap**: Marcus writes "educational, not salesy" outreach but lacks structured frameworks.
**Fix**: Apply cold-email skill frameworks:
- Use "Observation → Problem → Proof → Ask" for website audit offers
- Personalize based on actual site issues (weak Google presence, outdated design)
- Interest-based CTAs: "Want me to send over what I found?" vs. booking a call

#### 5. No Pricing Page Strategy (Skills: `pricing-strategy`, `page-cro`)
**Gap**: No mention of how agency services are packaged and presented.
**Fix**:
- Define clear service tiers (Website Build | Social Management | Full-Service + AI)
- Create a pricing page that handles "which is right for me?" anxiety
- Use page-cro framework for the services/pricing page

#### 6. Client Retention Needs Churn Prevention (Skill: `churn-prevention`)
**Gap**: Carlos monitors for at-risk clients reactively.
**Fix**: Apply churn-prevention skill's proactive framework:
- Define leading indicators of churn (engagement drop-off signals)
- Build automated health score based on client response times and results metrics
- Create structured save offers (pause, downgrade, switch services)

#### 7. No Competitor Positioning (Skill: `competitor-alternatives`)
**Gap**: No "Calling Digital vs. other agencies" content for consideration stage.
**Fix**: Create comparison content:
- "Calling Digital vs. Freelancers" — why an agency with AI capabilities wins
- "Full-Service Agency vs. Point Solutions" — the bundle advantage
- SEO-targeted "[City] digital marketing agency" comparison pages

## Business 3: Automotive Intelligence (Chase + Ryan Data + Atlas)

### What's Working
- Newsletter strategy ("What The Prompt?") is excellent for B2B trust-building
- Three-step sales funnel (Free Assessment → $2,500 → $7,500) is clean
- Chase↔Atlas collaboration (research → content) is strong
- Ryan Data's 200 touches/week with pipeline tracking

### Critical Gaps & Recommendations

#### 1. Cold Email Sequences Need Optimization (Skill: `cold-email`)
**Gap**: Chase writes cold email openers for Ryan Data but lacks proven cold email frameworks.
**Fix**: Apply the cold-email skill comprehensively:
- Use "Trigger → Insight → Ask" framework (dealer news → AI insight → free assessment offer)
- Subject lines: 2-4 words, lowercase, internal-looking ("your bdc response time")
- 5-email sequence with angle rotation: industry trend → competitor move → ROI data → case study → breakup
- Each follow-up stands alone (dealers may not have read previous emails)

#### 2. Newsletter Needs Email Sequence Infrastructure (Skill: `email-sequence`)
**Gap**: "What The Prompt?" newsletter exists but there's no subscriber nurture system.
**Fix**: Build sequences around the newsletter:
- **Welcome Sequence**: 5-email series for new subscribers (who you are → best insights → assessment offer)
- **Engagement-Based Trigger**: When a subscriber clicks 3+ links, trigger Ryan Data outreach
- **Re-Engagement**: 3-email series for subscribers who haven't opened in 30 days

#### 3. LinkedIn Strategy Needs Structure (Skill: `social-content`)
**Gap**: Chase creates LinkedIn posts for Michael Meta's personal brand but lacks a systematic content pillar framework.
**Fix**: Apply social-content skill's content pillars:
- 30% Industry Insights (AI trends in auto retail)
- 25% Behind-the-Scenes (building Automotive Intelligence)
- 25% Educational (frameworks dealers can use today)
- 15% Hot Takes (contrarian views on dealership AI)
- 5% Promotional (assessment offers)
- Daily engagement routine: 30 min commenting on dealer and auto industry posts

#### 4. No Lead Magnets for Dealers (Skill: `lead-magnets` + `free-tool-strategy`)
**Gap**: The free assessment IS the lead magnet, but there's no lower-commitment entry point.
**Fix**: Create tiered lead magnets:
- "AI Readiness Scorecard" — 2-minute self-assessment quiz that generates a score (`free-tool-strategy`)
- "The Dealer's Guide to AI in 2026" — comprehensive PDF (captures email, nurtures to assessment)
- "BDC Response Time Benchmark Report" — data-driven report dealers can compare against

#### 5. No SEO/AEO Content Strategy (Skills: `seo-audit`, `ai-seo`, `programmatic-seo`)
**Gap**: Chase targets "top 3 for 5 automotive AI keywords" but lacks structured SEO execution.
**Fix**:
- Run SEO audit on automotiveintelligence.com
- Target programmatic SEO: "AI for [dealer type] dealerships" pages at scale
- Optimize for AI search: structure content for LLM citation (ai-seo skill)
- Implement FAQ and Organization schema markup (`schema-markup`)

#### 6. No Sales Enablement Collateral (Skill: `sales-enablement`)
**Gap**: Ryan Data does outreach but no mention of structured sales materials.
**Fix**: Create using sales-enablement skill:
- One-pager: "What AI Can Do for Your Dealership in 90 Days"
- ROI calculator deck for the $2,500 audit pitch
- Objection library: "We already have a BDC," "AI will replace my staff," "We tried chatbots"
- Demo script for the free assessment call

#### 7. No Referral/Partner Program (Skill: `referral-program`)
**Gap**: No mechanism for satisfied dealers to refer other dealers.
**Fix**:
- Offer: $500 credit toward implementation for each referral that books an assessment
- Partner with auto industry consultants, DMS vendors, and dealer associations
- Create co-branded content with industry partners

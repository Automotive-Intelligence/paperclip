# Product Marketing Context

## Company Portfolio

This platform operates **three businesses** under a shared AI-powered agent system (Paperclip):

### 1. The AI Phone Guy
- **Product**: AI receptionist service for local service businesses
- **Market**: DFW area — HVAC, plumbing, dental, legal, roofing
- **Pricing**: $187/mo (Founder Offer) | $482/mo (Standard Rate)
- **Audience**: Skeptical small business owners worried about cost, missing calls, and losing revenue
- **Differentiator**: Hyper-local focus on DFW suburbs (Aubrey, Celina, Prosper, Pilot Point, Little Elm)
- **Voice**: Educational, relatable, plain-language. No jargon.

### 2. Calling Digital
- **Product**: Digital marketing agency + AI implementation consulting
- **Market**: Dallas-area SMBs needing websites, social media, and AI solutions
- **Model**: Website/social retainer → AI Phone Guy bundle upsell → AI consulting (Nova)
- **Audience**: Business owners who know they need digital help but haven't articulated the problem
- **Differentiator**: Full-service digital + AI consulting bundle
- **Voice**: On-brand, purposeful, conversion-oriented

### 3. Automotive Intelligence
- **Product**: AI readiness consulting for car dealerships
- **Market**: DFW and national auto retail
- **Pricing**: Free Assessment → $2,500 Audit → $7,500 Implementation
- **Audience**: Dealer Principals and GMs thinking about AI but skeptical of vendors
- **Differentiator**: Industry insiders, not tech outsiders. Clarity over hype.
- **Voice**: Authoritative, educational, never hype-driven
